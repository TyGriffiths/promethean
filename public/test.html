Beliefs

Because Education Is More Than Just Knowing Stuff - It's About What Inspires You

Empowered Learners

We believe in learning what you want at the pace you want and getting credit for it, regardless of how and where you learn it.

Empowered Teachers

We believe in teachers empowered with the flexibility, compensation, and teaching style they dictate.

Empowered Employers

We believe in employers knowing exactly what an applicant is capable of and who act as architects within learning paths.

Evidence Learning

We believe in learning which is proven through transparency and application. If you can prove it, you deserve it.

The Future of Teachers

We believe in teachers as resources when you need them at the price you want them, with the ability for anyone to teach.

The Learning Revolution

We believe in empowerment for all by challenging the status quo.

Learning Process

6 Steps


Create Profile

To gain access to life-changing opportunities.

Search Badges

Find the skills or knowledge you want to acquire.

Enroll in Badge

Click the "Enroll" button at the top right of any badge page.

Upload Proof of Competency

Any file or URL that proves you know what you're learning is great!

Connect with Teachers as Needed

For a matter of minutes, months, or maybe life. They'll get you where you're needing to go.

Request Badge Approval

When you've proven competency in a badge's criteria a teacher or Promethean can approve your badge.

Teaching Process

4 Steps


Create Profile

To gain access to life-changing opportunities.

Accept Learner Requests

You'll see them coming. Just approve them and get in contact with them.

Provide Instruction

In whatever you way feel best meets the learner's needs. Feel free to change the world.

Approve Badges

Once a learner has proven competency in a badge's criteria you can approve that badge.
