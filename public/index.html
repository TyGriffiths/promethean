Beliefs

Because Education Is More Than Just Knowing Stuff - It's About What Inspires You

Empowered Learners

We believe in learning what you want at the pace you want and getting credit for it, regardless of how and where you learn it.

Empowered Teachers

We believe in teachers empowered with the flexibility, compensation, and teaching style they dictate.

Empowered Employers

We believe in employers knowing exactly what an applicant is capable of and who act as architects within learning paths.

Evidence Learning

We believe in learning which is proven through transparency and application. If you can prove it, you deserve it.

The Future of Teachers

We believe in teachers as resources when you need them at the price you want them, with the ability for anyone to teach.

The Learning Revolution

We believe in empowerment for all by challenging the status quo.
